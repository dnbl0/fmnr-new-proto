Farmer-Managed Natural Regeneration is the most cost-effective and rapidly scalable land restoration technique known. By working with the natural regrowth capacity already present in the soil, FMNR can transform degraded farmland in a matter of years — not decades.

The 1-Billion Hectare Vision is not a distant dream. It is a strategic goal underpinned by proven methodology, growing practitioner networks, and mounting evidence of ecosystem, food security, and climate outcomes.
