Humbo — Community Restoration in Ethiopia

Humbo Community Reforestation Programme (ERPA) — a large-scale restoration effort that demonstrates FMNR's ability to regenerate woodlands and improve livelihoods across landscape scales.

Overview

The Humbo reforestation project is a landmark example of community-led restoration in Ethiopia. Local communities, supported by partners, have restored degraded lands through FMNR and guided natural regeneration.
