Why give

One of the most cost-effective ways to restore the planet

FMNR works with the trees already in the ground. By protecting and managing natural regrowth rather than planting new seedlings, communities can regreen vast areas of degraded land for a fraction of the cost of conventional restoration — while improving food security, building climate resilience, and creating lasting livelihoods.
