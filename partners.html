Why partner with FMNR

Built for Scale. Designed for Impact.

FMNR is uniquely suited to large-scale partnership because it is farmer-led, low-cost, and generates compounding returns over time.

Scale

Proven at Every Scale

From a single farm to millions of hectares — FMNR has been validated across 24+ countries, with peer-reviewed evidence of landscape, livelihood and climate outcomes at national scale.

Simplicity

No Special Inputs Required

FMNR relies on the knowledge and agency of farmers — no seeds, no seedlings, no external inputs. This makes it resilient, affordable and transferable across diverse agro-ecological contexts.

Evidence

Measurable Returns

FMNR generates documented improvements in crop yields, income, biodiversity, carbon sequestration and water availability — with satellite monitoring and farmer-reported data available to partners.
