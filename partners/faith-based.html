How we partner

Restoring Land and Communities Through Faith

Faith communities are uniquely positioned to drive lasting change — their moral authority, local trust and deep networks reach where other institutions cannot. We equip faith leaders to integrate FMNR into their ministry and mobilise their congregations.

Mobilisation

Community Engagement

Workshops, sermons and demonstration plots help faith communities understand and adopt FMNR. Restoration becomes embedded in the rhythms of congregational life — not a separate project, but an expression of faith.

Training

Leader Resources

Faith-adapted training packs, guides and sermon outlines let pastors and imams integrate land stewardship into their teaching. Materials are designed for local languages and oral traditions across Sub-Saharan Africa and beyond.

Networks

Faith Network Reach

We work with the World Council of Churches and regional faith networks to scale FMNR rapidly across denominations and traditions — amplifying local champions through connected institutions.

01

Walk With Your Community

Faith leaders in FMNR programmes don't just teach restoration techniques — they model them. Leaders like Gebregziebeher visit local churches every Sunday, weaving FMNR into the message of creation care and farming households into a network of practice. We help faith partners develop this local champion model and embed it into their pastoral rhythms.

  • Landscape assessment with local faith leaders
  • Integration into existing pastoral programmes
  • Identification of farmer champions within congregations
  • Demonstration plot establishment and Sunday teaching

02

Train & Multiply Faith Champions

One trained faith leader can reach hundreds of households across a congregation — and through their networks, thousands more. We provide structured training, materials and ongoing support so that faith-based FMNR champions can multiply the movement across their denomination and geography without ongoing dependence on outside expertise.

  • Certified faith leader FMNR training
  • Train-the-trainer programmes across denominations
  • Faith-adapted monitoring and accountability frameworks
  • Network convening and inter-denomination exchange
