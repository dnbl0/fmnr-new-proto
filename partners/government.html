How we partner

From Policy to Practice

We work with national and sub-national governments to translate FMNR's proven evidence base into policy frameworks, extension delivery and results-based incentive structures that work for smallholder farmers.

Policy

Policy Integration

Technical advisory support to embed FMNR in national restoration strategies, NDCs, land-use frameworks and forestry legislation — with implementation roadmaps your team can act on.

Extension

Extension System Support

Strengthen government extension capacity to deliver FMNR at scale — including training curricula for field officers, contextualised manuals and community facilitation guides.

Finance

Incentives & Finance

Guidance on financing mechanisms and community incentive structures — from results-based payments and carbon finance to budget integration and land tenure reforms.

01

Assess Policy Landscape

We start by mapping existing policy frameworks, extension systems and restoration commitments to identify where FMNR integration creates the greatest leverage — without duplicating existing structures.

  • Policy gap analysis and mapping
  • Extension system capacity assessment
  • Stakeholder and champion identification
  • Landscape prioritisation support

02

Build & Deliver

Once the policy entry points are identified, we co-develop implementation plans, training materials and monitoring systems — supporting government teams to deliver FMNR through their own structures at scale.

  • Extension officer training programmes
  • Community facilitation toolkits
  • Satellite-based landscape monitoring
  • Annual progress reporting
