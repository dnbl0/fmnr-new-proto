Practical Support for Programme Teams

Design

Programme Integration

Co-design FMNR activities that align with your log frames, theory of change and donor outcome requirements — so FMNR strengthens your programme rather than duplicating it.

Capacity

Field Training & Tools

Evidence

Monitoring & Evaluation

01

Assess & Align

We start by reviewing your programme context — target communities, geography, existing activities and donor requirements — to identify the most effective way to integrate FMNR without disrupting what's working.

  • Programme context and landscape review
  • FMNR potential mapping
  • Donor outcome alignment
  • Community readiness assessment

02

Train & Scale

Once integrated, we provide practical training for your field teams and community facilitators, along with monitoring tools to track adoption and impact — giving you the data needed for donor reporting and programme adaptation.

  • Field officer and facilitator training
  • Localised training materials
  • Satellite and field monitoring setup
  • Quarterly impact reporting templates
